This has pulled a total of nine (very distinct) jobs from jobs.disneycareers.com. We manually created variables that apply to the roles themselves. For example, in our Sr. Marketing Insights Analyst role, we require that the applicant answered yes that they have advanced technological skills, whereas the Housekeeping - Part-Time, Disney's Vero Beach Resort does not require that particular aspect.

Naturally, when the site is live and in practice, the survey will be more expansive and the available roles that are applicable will be delivered in a more efficient way. This is a prototype of the functionality of the website aspect.

To create this, surveyjs.io was used to create a survey that is expandable and easy to manage. With the simplicity of this technology, one of the technical hurdles of this aspect has been mediated.
